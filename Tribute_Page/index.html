Swami Vivekananda

The man of mordern spirituality

Vivekananda was a Hindu spiritual leader who attempted to combine Indian spirituality with Western material progress.

Timeline

  • 1863 Born Narendranath Dutta in Calcutta, India.
  • 1881 Meets Sri Ramakrishna, a Hindu mystic and saint.
  • 1884 Graduates from Presidency College in Calcutta.
  • 1886 Takes formal vows of sannyasa (renunciation) and becomes Swami Vivekananda.
  • 1890 Begins traveling all over India as an itinerant monk.
  • 1892 Visits Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of India.
  • 1893 Travels to the United States to attend the World's Parliament of Religions in Chicago.
  • 1894 Founds the Vedanta Society of New York.
  • 1895 Founds the Ramakrishna Mission in India.
  • 1896 Returns to India after three years in the West.
  • 1897 Establishes the Belur Math, the headquarters of the Ramakrishna Mission.
  • 1900 Travels to Europe and the Middle East.
  • 1902 Dies in Belur Math at the age of 39.
